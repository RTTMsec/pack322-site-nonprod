Getting Started with Cub Scouts

Welcome to Pack 322! We're excited to have you join our Cub Scout family. Here are some key resources and information to help you get started:

Step 1: Registration

Register online through the my.scouting.org portal using the link below:

Join Pack 322 – Click here to Register Online!

Step 2: Uniform & Supplies

All new Scouts will need to purchase a uniform and some basic supplies. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Official Cub Scout uniform: shirt, belt, and blue pants/shorts. For ranks other than lions, you will also need a neckerchief and slide.
  • Handbook for your Scout's rank.

    • For additional information on uniforms and supplies, please visit our Uniforms page.

Step 3: Pay Pack Dues

Pack dues help cover the costs of activities, supplies, and events throughout the year. The annual dues for Pack 322 are $88 per Scout.

You can pay dues online through the following link:

Pay Pack Dues Online

Step 4: Attend Meetings & Events

Our Pack meetings are held on the 2nd Thursday and Den meetings are held on the 4th Thursday of each month.

Additional details of events and activities will be communicated via email and our website. You can find the full calendar of events here.
