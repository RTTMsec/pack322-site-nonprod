Cub Scout Uniform Requirements

Lion - Kindergarten Tiger - First Grade Wolf - Second Grade Bear - Third Grade Webelos - Fourth Grade Arrow of Light - Fifth Grade

Scouting America provides a listing of essential gear for Cub Scouts:

  • Lions: Only a shirt and belt are required.
  • Tiger and Beyond: Cub Scouts should have a shirt, neckerchief, slide, and belt. Official pants, socks, and hat are optional, but encouraged.
