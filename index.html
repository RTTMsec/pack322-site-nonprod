Cub Scout Pack 322 serves the Eureka, Missouri, and surrounding communities, offering scouting programs to both boys and girls from kindergarten through fifth grade. We are part of the New Horizons District in the Greater St. Louis Council and are sponsored by The River.

Pack Meetings

Pack Meetings are scheduled for the second Thursday of the month at 6:30PM and Outdoor Pack Adventures are scheduled for the third Saturday of the month. Information for Pack Meeting and Outdoor Pack Adventures are maintained in the Cub Scout Calendar.

At Pack 322, we believe that Cub Scouting is about more than just earning badges—it's about building a strong sense of community and belonging. Our pack provides a welcoming environment where scouts and their families can form lasting friendships, learn important life skills, and grow together. Through hands-on activities, outdoor adventures, and teamwork, our scouts develop confidence, leadership, and a love for nature. Whether we're hiking, camping, or working on service projects, we strive to create memorable experiences that inspire curiosity, kindness, and a spirit of adventure in every scout. Join us as we explore, learn, and have fun with like-minded friends!
